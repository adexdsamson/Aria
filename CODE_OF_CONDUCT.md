# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), version 2.1, as its code of conduct. The full text is available at that link and is incorporated here by reference.

In short: everyone who participates in this project — through issues, pull requests, discussions, or any other channel — is expected to help keep it a respectful, harassment-free, and welcoming space, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in language and actions.
- Offer and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and the community.
- Show empathy toward other community members.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, personal attacks, demeaning or discriminatory remarks, publishing others' private information without consent, and other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

This is a solo-maintainer project. Concerns about conduct can be reported privately to the maintainer at [adexdsamson@gmail.com](mailto:adexdsamson@gmail.com). Reports will be reviewed and handled discreetly. The maintainer may take any action deemed appropriate, including warnings or removal from project spaces, up to and including a permanent ban for serious or repeated violations.

For the full enforcement guidelines and the community-impact ladder, see the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
