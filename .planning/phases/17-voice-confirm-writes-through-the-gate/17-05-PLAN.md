| VOICE_CONFIRM_APPROVAL → voiceConfirm → assertApproved | voiceConfirm stamps 'voice-explicit'; assertApproved in write chokepoints enforces HARD GATE for forced/high-severity |

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |

| T-17-13 | Tampering | Hedged confirm utterance auto-executing | mitigate | generateObject ConfirmIntentSchema.ambiguous → re-prompt max 2 → auto-cancel. Never execute on 'ambiguous'. |
